About Me

Hello! My name is Kevin Zvonarek. I am a third-year at The University of Michigan currently pursuing a Physics and Mathematics major through the College of Literature, Science, and the Arts.
This year has been very challenging and especially so for K-12 students. I am working to provide free and accessible tutoring, seminars, and workshops to students in underserved communities virtually. I am very passionate about creating inclusive learning environments; starting from the ground up.
