Outreach

Current Projects

  • As of January 2021, I am working with the U-M Physics Department's Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Committee, U-M's Society of Physics Students, and the Jalen Rose Leadership Academy (JRLA) in Detroit, MI to connect undergraduate volunteers with understaffed high school physics classes.

  • Volunteers are tasked with helping students work through their classroom activities; such as virtual simulations using "PHET Interactive Simulations" provided by the University of Colorado Boulder.

  • Additionally, I am working with these organizations in partnership with U-M's recruiting offices to launch a program where JRLA students can visit U-M's Ann Arbor campus and shadow a physics student or faculty member; starting this Fall 2021 semester. This shadowing program aims at demonstrating to eager high school students what studying and researching physics at the college level can look like.

  • I am very passionate about creating inclusive learning environments; starting from the ground up. The COVID-19 Pandemic has brought my in-person commitments to serving high school students to the virtual domain and since mid 2020 I have been creating free and accesible workshops aimed at empowering and educating students around the nation; available in both Spanish and English.

  • With JRLA specifically, I plan on bringing my informative presentations to non-graduating students regarding course selection that will best prepare them for their intended major, breaking down the college application process, and more.

About Me

Hello! My name is Kevin Zvonarek. I am a third-year at the University of Michigan currently pursuing a Physics and Mathematics major through the College of Literature, Science, and the Arts.

Given that I come from an immigrant family I make sure to create bilingual content so that all of my information is accesible. I have been tutoring for 5 years in subjects ranging from standardized tests (ACT, SAT, PSAT) to AP/IB courses. My virtual drop-in rooms act as an office hour where students in middle school and high school can come to discuss topics such as: applying to college, working while in school, establishing sustainable and healthy lifestyles, personal finance, etc.

Podcasts I am currently listen to include: "Eigenbros" with Juan and Terence and "HumanAIn" with David Yakobovitch

On weekends I can be found catching up on SNL Skits, "The Daily Show" with Trevor Noah, and watching the latest episode of "Last Week Tonight" with John Oliver.

A good friend of mine recently introduced me to the author Tim James. I am reading through his latest novels "Astronomical" and "Fundamental".

I always make time to exercise at my nearby reservoir and practice speedcubing. Pre-pandemic you would have found me traveling between both coasts and exploring SoCal with my girlfriend and her younger brother.
